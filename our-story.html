Practical Digital Solutions for Real Organizations

Hinrichs Specialty Services and Technology provides practical digital solutions for small businesses, nonprofits, and service organizations across Nebraska and beyond. Led by Brandon Hinrichs, a digital strategist with over 15 years of experience in systems design and organizational development, the company helps clients modernize their operations through thoughtful use of technology.

Our work focuses on creating sustainable, people-centered digital tools — from website development and SEO optimization to workflow automation and integrated analytics. Every project is designed to enhance visibility, simplify operations, and strengthen long-term growth.

We believe technology should be approachable, ethical, and genuinely useful. By combining strategic insight with hands-on support, Hinrichs Specialty Services and Technology helps organizations build systems that work smarter, communicate clearly, and adapt confidently to change.
