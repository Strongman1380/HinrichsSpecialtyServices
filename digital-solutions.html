Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for a project?

You'll receive a secure invoice link via email. Fixed-price packages can also be initiated with a deposit paid directly from the pricing page above. All payments are processed online.

Are services invoiced separately?

Yes. Each service or project gets its own itemized invoice. Hourly work is invoiced upon completion with a full task breakdown. No bundled subscriptions — you pay only for what you use.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Projects over $1,500 can be split 50% at start and 50% at delivery. For larger database or custom projects, milestone-based invoicing is available.

How quickly can you get started?

Typically within 5 business days of deposit receipt. Smaller tasks (Basic/Standard hourly work) are often started within 1–2 days.

Do I own the files when we're done?

Absolutely. Upon final payment, you receive all files, code, credentials, and content. No vendor lock-in, ever.

Do you work outside Nebraska?

Yes — while we're based in Geneva, Nebraska, we serve clients nationwide through a fully remote, digital-first approach.
